The Open Racing Vision

Founded by Augustin Boulet

Open Racing isn't just another racing school; it’s a revolution in motorsport accessibility. I created this academy because I saw too many talented drivers stopped by financial or logistical barriers before they could even prove themselves.

Our concept is simple but elite: we provide high-level, 100% 2-stroke competition karting packages across Europe's most challenging tracks. By centralization, optimizing logistics, and focusing strictly on performance coaching, we reduce the entry cost for serious drivers by up to 40%.

From engine tuning to professional telemetry analysis, Open Racing gives you the tools to succeed. We don't just put you on the grid; we prepare you to dominate it.

Augustin Boulet, Founder
